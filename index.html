What we offer

Embedded training

We work directly with software teams to observe, recognize patterns, and introduce more effective practices.

Over the course of a release cycle or sprint, we spend half a day pairing with each team member, observing and guiding.

At the end of a cycle, we synthesize our observations and make further recommendations.

This can begin as an exploratory exercise or with a direct goal in mind, e.g. improving observability, deployment frequency, code review quality.

Facilitation & workshops

As experienced teachers and trainers, we have developed workshops on a variety of engineering topics, such as:

  • Iterative product development for engineers
  • Using curiosity to bridge communications gaps
  • Practical introduction to trunk-based development
  • Effective incident response
  • Facilitated team and project retrospectives
  • Facilitated kick-offs

Professional coaching

We are skilled and expert listeners who can promote professional development.

  • Ongoing 1:1 coaching
  • Facilitated peer networking (e.g. people managers)
  • Facilitated manager <> report 1:1
  • Mentoring an individual or team through a transition, e.g. first management role

Organizational setup & transformation

Backed by research and our experience as leaders, we assist with the creation and maintenance of effective organizational structures in engineering.

We consult with senior management about measures of success and perceived gaps.

We devise metrics and suggest targets that will confirm success.

We investigate the organization to determine the current state of these metrics by conducting interviews and inspecting existing tooling.

We suggest remediations, which we can support organizations to implement.
